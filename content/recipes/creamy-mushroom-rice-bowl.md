Servings: 1-2 Enjoy your delicious and comforting Creamy Mushroom Rice Bowl!

### Ingredients

1 cup black rice
2 cups water
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion, finely chopped (no nightshades!)
3 cloves garlic, minced
8 oz mixed mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and button), sliced
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup milk (diabetic-friendly)
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon dried oregano
Salt and black pepper, to taste
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (low potassium)
Fresh thyme leaves, for garnish (optional)

### Process

1. **Prepare the rice:** Rinse the black rice in a fine-mesh strainer until the water runs clear. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed rice and 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 40-45 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed.
2. **Sauté the onion and garlic:** In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until it's translucent and starting to caramelize (about 5-7 minutes). Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly to prevent burning.
3. **Add the mushrooms:** Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet with the onion mixture. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms release their moisture and start to brown (about 5-7 minutes).
4. **Make the creamy sauce:** In a small saucepan, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour to make a roux, cooking for 1 minute. Gradually whisk in the milk, bringing the mixture to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens (about 5-7 minutes). Remove from heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
5. **Assemble the bowl:** Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork to separate the grains. Divide the cooked rice between two bowls (or store for later use). Top each bowl with the sautéed mushroom mixture, then spoon the creamy sauce over the mushrooms. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves, if desired.

### Notes

For a creamier sauce, you can add 1-2 tablespoons of heavy cream or half-and-half to the milk mixture.
To reduce potassium levels further, omit the black rice and serve the mushroom mixture over cooked white rice instead.
You can also use this recipe as a base and add your favorite protein sources, such as tofu or cooked chicken, for added variety.
Enjoy your delicious and comforting Creamy Mushroom Rice Bowl!
