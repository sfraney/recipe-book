## Ingredients

* 1–1.5 lb cooked chicken, chopped
* 12 oz egg noodles
* 2 cups mushrooms
* ½ onion
* 2 tbsp butter
* 2 tbsp flour
* 2 cups milk

## Instructions

1. Cook noodles until slightly underdone.
2. Cook mushrooms and onion.
3. Make béchamel: melt butter, add flour, whisk in milk.
4. Combine all ingredients.
5. Bake at 375°F for 25–30 minutes.
