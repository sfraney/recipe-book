## Ingredients

* 1 lb cooked chicken, shredded
* 4 cups water or broth
* 1 tbsp ginger
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1 cup carrot + celery
* 1–2 cups cabbage
* Cooked grain mix (to serve)
* Soy sauce
* Sesame oil

## Instructions

1. Simmer broth with ginger and garlic.
2. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
3. Add chicken and heat through.
4. Serve with a small scoop of grain mix per bowl.
5. Finish with soy sauce and sesame oil.
