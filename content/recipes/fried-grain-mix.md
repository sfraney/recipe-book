## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked grain mix (black rice + lentils + quinoa), cold
- 1–2 eggs
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, celery, etc.)
- 1–2 cups cooked protein (chicken, pork, or tofu)
- 2–3 tbsp oil
- 1–2 tbsp soy sauce (use less than standard fried rice)
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1–2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, minced

---

## Method

### 1. Cook Eggs
Heat pan with oil. Scramble eggs lightly. Remove and set aside.

### 2. Aromatics
Add garlic and ginger. Stir-fry briefly until fragrant.

### 3. Vegetables
Add vegetables and cook until just tender.

### 4. Add Grains
Add grain mix. Break up clumps and heat through.

### 5. Add Protein
Add cooked protein and stir to combine.

### 6. Season
Add soy sauce sparingly. Toss to distribute.

### 7. Finish
Return eggs. Add sesame oil. Toss and serve.

---

## Notes

- Use less sauce than traditional fried rice to avoid sogginess.
- Do not over-stir; the grain mix is softer than rice.
- This is a secondary component; keep portions moderate.
