## Ingredients

### Soup

* 4 cups water or broth
* 1 tbsp ginger
* 2 tbsp soy sauce
* 3 eggs, beaten

### Fried Grain Mix

* 2 cups cooked grain mix
* 1 cup mixed vegetables
* 1 egg
* Soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Simmer broth with ginger and soy.
2. Slowly drizzle in eggs while stirring.
3. For grains: sauté vegetables, add grains.
4. Push aside, scramble egg, mix together.
5. Season and serve together.
