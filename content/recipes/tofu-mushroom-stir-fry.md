## Ingredients

* 1 block firm tofu, cubed
* 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
* 1–2 heads bok choy
* 1 tbsp ginger
* 2 cloves garlic
* 2–3 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tbsp rice or black vinegar
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* Neutral oil

## Instructions

1. Pan-fry tofu in oil until golden; remove.
2. Cook mushrooms until browned.
3. Add bok choy, cook briefly.
4. Add ginger and garlic.
5. Return tofu, add soy and vinegar.
6. Finish with sesame oil.
