## Ingredients

* 1 lb chicken thighs, thin sliced
* 3 cups cabbage, sliced
* 1 carrot, sliced
* 1–2 stalks celery, sliced
* 1 tbsp ginger, minced
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 2–3 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tbsp rice vinegar
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* Neutral oil

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Add chicken and cook until mostly done.
3. Add vegetables and stir fry 3–5 minutes.
4. Add ginger and garlic; cook briefly.
5. Add soy sauce and vinegar; toss.
6. Finish with sesame oil and serve.
