## Ingredients

### Base
- 1½ cups cooked grain mix (black rice + lentils + quinoa)
- ¾–1 lb chicken thighs

### Vegetables
- ½ cup carrot, sliced
- ½ cup celery, sliced
- ¼–⅓ onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tsp ginger, minced

### Binder / Liquid
- ⅓–½ cup chicken broth (use less for firmer texture)
- 1 egg, beaten

### Flavor
- 2 tsp soy sauce
- ½ tsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp neutral oil

---

## Method

### 1. Preheat
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly oil a small casserole dish.

### 2. Build Base
In a bowl, combine:
- cooked grain mix  
- vegetables  
- garlic and ginger  
- broth  
- soy sauce and vinegar  
- beaten egg  

Mix thoroughly.

### 3. Add Chicken
Nestle chicken into the mixture, leaving some surface exposed.

Optional:
- Sear chicken briefly first for better flavor.

### 4. Bake
Bake uncovered for 35–40 minutes, until:
- chicken is fully cooked  
- top is lightly browned  
- mixture is set  

### 5. Rest
Rest 5–10 minutes before slicing.

---

## Notes

- Use **closer to ⅓ cup broth** if you want a clearly sliceable result.
- This yields ~2 solid portions or 2 + leftovers.
- Works best in a smaller dish (8x8 or similar) to maintain thickness.

---

## Make Ahead

- Assemble up to 24 hours ahead (add a splash of broth before baking).
- Keeps 3–4 days refrigerated after cooking.
