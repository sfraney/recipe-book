## Ingredients

* 1.5–2 lb chicken thighs
* 2 cups cooked grain mix
* 1 cup carrot, chopped
* 1 cup celery, chopped
* ½ onion, chopped
* 2 cups broth or water
* 2 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tbsp ginger

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Combine all ingredients in a baking dish.
3. Cover and bake 45–60 minutes until chicken is cooked.
4. Rest 5–10 minutes before serving.
