## Ingredients

- 4 cups chicken broth
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- ½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)
- ¼ tsp sugar
- ⅛ tsp white pepper (optional)
- 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water (slurry)
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 scallion, chopped (optional)
- ½ tsp grated ginger

---

## Method

### 1. Heat Broth
Bring chicken broth to a simmer. Add ginger, salt, sugar, and white pepper.

### 2. Thicken
Stir in cornstarch slurry and simmer until slightly thickened.

### 3. Add Eggs
Reduce heat to low. Slowly drizzle beaten eggs into the soup while stirring gently to create ribbons.

### 4. Finish
Add sesame oil and scallions. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- Stirring speed controls egg texture (slow = larger ribbons).
- Keep heat low when adding eggs to avoid breaking them up too much.
- This is the primary volume component of the meal.
