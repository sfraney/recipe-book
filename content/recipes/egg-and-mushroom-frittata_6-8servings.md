Servings: 6-8 Enjoy your delicious Egg and Mushroom Frittata!

### Ingredients

- 12 large eggs
- 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy, such as almond or soy milk)
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 cups mixed mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and button), sliced
- 2 sprigs fresh oregano, chopped
- 1 sprig fresh thyme, chopped
- 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

### Process

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs and milk until well combined.
3. Add melted butter, olive oil, chopped oregano, and chopped thyme to the egg mixture. Whisk until smooth.
4. Heat a 9-inch (23 cm) non-stick skillet or omelette pan over medium-high heat.
5. Add sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they release their moisture and start browning.
6. Pour the egg mixture over the mushrooms in the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the edges start to set.
7. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the top of the frittata.
8. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the cheese is melted and golden brown.
9. Remove the frittata from the oven and let it cool in the skillet for 2-3 minutes.
10. Use a spatula to carefully loosen the edges of the frittata from the skillet.
11. Slide the frittata onto a serving plate or cutting board.
12. Slice into wedges and serve warm.

### Notes

To ensure a smooth, fluffy frittata, make sure to whisk the egg mixture well and cook it over medium heat until the edges start to set.
Don't overcrowd the skillet when adding mushrooms; cook them in batches if necessary.
Use a non-stick skillet or omelette pan to prevent the frittata from sticking and to make removal easier.
If you prefer a more golden-brown top, broil the frittata for an additional 1-2 minutes after baking. Keep an eye on it to avoid burning.
Enjoy your delicious Egg and Mushroom Frittata!
