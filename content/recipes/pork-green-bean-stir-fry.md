## Ingredients

* 1 lb ground or sliced pork
* 1 lb green beans
* 3 cloves garlic
* 2 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tsp sugar (optional)
* Neutral oil

## Instructions

1. Cook pork in oil until browned.
2. Add green beans and cook until tender.
3. Add garlic and cook briefly.
4. Add soy sauce and optional sugar.
5. Stir and serve.
